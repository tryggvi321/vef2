Yu-Gi-Oh

Yu-Gi-Oh is a card game created in 1999 by konami and still gets packs every month. Yu-Gi-Oh is a game where both players start with 8000 life points and the goal is to make your opponent lose all theyre lifepoints mostly by attacking with monsters. the game has 3 types of cards traps spells and monsters

MAGIC-THE-GATHERING

MAGIC-THE-GATHERING is a game where your goal is to make your opponent lose all theyr life points just like in Yu-Gi-Oh but instead you only have 20 life points you do this by attackign with units you summon units however cannot attack the same turn they are summoned. you summon units by using energy you gain from resource cards.

Yu-Gi-Oh MAGIC-THE-GATHERING

Yu-Gi-Ohs card limit is 40-60 cards while magic has no card limit so make sure your card deck isnt to big or to small magic is easier to learn then Yu-Gi-Oh so if your just starting out we would reccomend trying magic out first then giving Yu-Gi-Oh a try if you enjoy playing card games.
